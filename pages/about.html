About

I believe that willingness to be a fool is the precursor to transformation. Now learning require you to be a fool, first. Here is where I put my notes on understanding the nature of learning right from the perspective of a fool -- that at the beginning I am/was (depending on perspective of what I'm learning).

I got inspired to pursue towards the ML by people like Andrew Ng who not only are experts in ML, but also makes all the best efforts to democratize this area.

It is fascinating how AI becomes an art of modern days. AI expert should be a polymath to embrace the concept of mind. The AI brings together the philosophy, psychology, neuroscience and neurobiology.Being a modern AI expert is like being a science artist who knows how to optimally apply AI to a problem. I am fascinated by the AI and its possibilities. This blog aims to contain some amazing ideas that I have approached considered special :)

But after all, first you need to be a fool... to start the endeavor and to be in-formed of new.

I will try to explain things that I learn however, everything can be explained in two ways:

Look how clever I am

or

Look how easy it is

I always do my best not to get tempted by the first option ;) If you find the explanations to complex please let me know what you think.

Please feel free to contact me if you have a suggestion on present content or would like me to write on some specific topic. I am always also open to work on some nifty new ideas.

About Michał Chromiak
Awarded PhD in 2016 by Polish Academy of Sciences in Computer Science. Focus research on understanding chaos of data. Deeply understanding the phenomena makes it easy, but first you need to learn. Holds two titles of MSc in Mathematics and in Computer Science (Maria Curie-Skłodowska Univ.)
